Hot Chocolate

Hot chocolate is the best winter drink. This particular recipe has just the right amount of sweetness. Top off with some mini marshmallow or drink it as is.

Ingredients

  • White Sugar 50g
  • Cocoa 75g
  • Hot water 50 ml
  • Milk 200ml
  • mini marshmallow

Instructions

  1. Mix together the sugar and cocoa.
  2. Add the hot water and mix to form a paste
  3. Heat up the milk and add to the cocoa mix
  4. Top with marshmallows and enjoy!

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Nothing beats a classic chocolate chip cookie. These cookies are soft and chewy with just the right amount of crunch around the edges. They are a perfect afternoon treat with a cup of tea.

Ingredients

  • White Sugar 50g
  • Brown Sugar 75g
  • Butter 80g
  • Flour 140g
  • Egg 1
  • Baking soda 1 teaspoon
  • Chocolate chunks 100g

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the melted butter and sugars, then whisk in an egg.
  2. Add in the flour and baking soda, mix well
  3. Fold in the chocolate chunks, dont overmix.
  4. Let dough rest in fridge for half an hour
  5. Preheat oven to 170 degrees celcius
  6. Divide dough into ten parts and roll into balls
  7. Place on baking sheet and bake for about 15-18 minutes.
